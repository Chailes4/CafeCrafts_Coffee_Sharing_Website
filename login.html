Our Story

In the heart of a college town, three students, Chailes, Joza, and Den, bonded over their shared love for coffee. Inspired by their passion and fueled by countless late-night study sessions fueled by caffeine, they decided to create a platform where coffee enthusiasts could gather and share their favorite recipes. Thus, "Café Crafts" was born. With Chailes' knack for web design, Joza's expertise in coffee brewing, and Den's flair for storytelling, they crafted an inviting online space where users could exchange recipes, brewing techniques, and coffee-related experiences. Together, they embarked on a mission to build a vibrant community of coffee lovers, united by their love for the perfect cup.

Hot Mocha Latte

A mocha latte is a creamy, indulgent hot drink that has become hugely popular in recent years. The coffee is made with chocolate sauce, milk, robust espresso coffee, and whipped cream!

Coffee Capuccino

Cappuccino is one of the most popular and comforting coffee drinks. It contains equal amounts of espresso and steamed milk and is topped with a frothy foam layer.

Iced Americano Latte

An iced latte is made with espresso shots and milk. Both coffees use ice cubes for that refreshing touch.

Espresso

Espresso is definitely stronger than a latte. An espresso is a concentrated shot of coffee.
